Local Picks You Shouldn’t Miss.

In Paris, you might start your day with a warm croissant by the Seine, wander into a hidden bookstore that feels like a step back in time, and wrap up the night with a glass of wine under the Eiffel Tower’s glow.

Mamiche Bakery

Grab a flaky croissant or loaf of fresh sourdough at one of the city’s best bakeries—believe the hype, it’s worth the wait!

La Bellevilloise

Rooted in a rich cooperative past, this lively venue hosts concerts, art shows, and dance parties—while also serving up a restaurant and brunch—blending old-school spirit with a fresh, modern twist.

Outdoor Cinéma at La Villette

If you’re here in summer, catch a movie under the stars at Parc de la Villette—it’s a chill way to soak up the city’s cultural vibe.
